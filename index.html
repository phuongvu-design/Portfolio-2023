About me

I'm a UI/UX designer based in Massachusetts. My background in graphic design and visual design skills complement my UX skills and my ability to create dynamic wireframes and prototypes. As a UI/UX designer, I seek to understand the needs of my stakeholders both the business side and users' side to provide the best experience possible. I am interested in UI/UX positions such as user experience, user interface, and user research positions. In addition, I'm willing to explore any industries that require my skillset.

I have worked on several projects involving solving problems for my clients through logo design, web design, and UI/UX designs. I have a strong background education in Design. I hold an Associate Degree from Bunker Hill Community College and a Bachelor's Degree in Communication Design from Massachusetts College of Art and Design. Thus, I have a solid knowledge of working with many design tools including Adobe Creative Suites, Adobe XD, Figma, and more... I enjoy playing games or taking photos. I also love brewing coffee and sharing my love for coffee with everyone around me.
