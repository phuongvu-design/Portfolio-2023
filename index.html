About me

I'm a graphic and UI/UX designer with a strong educational background. I hold a Bachelor of Arts in Communication Design from the Massachusetts College of Art and Design and an Associate of Arts in Visual Design from Bunker Hill Community College. I have honed my skills in design and am knowledgeable in industry standards.

I have a diverse range of design experience, including working as a freelance graphic designer, Listing Manager, and Curator. I have executed design solutions across various channels and media and have worked with clients to meet their design needs. Additionally, Phuong has completed a UI/UX Design Career Track Certificate from Springboard, where she gained over 700 hours of hands-on training in user experience design, prototyping, wireframes, and more. With a passion for design and a keen eye for detail, I am always a driven individual who is constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities to further develop my skills.
